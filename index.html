About Me

KBT is a Kyiv-based musician and producer who crafts atmospheric, multi-layered compositions, blending neo-soul, trip-hop, electro-house, and disco. His music moves between sensuality and rhythmic drive, spanning a wide range of tempos.

KBT’s performances are a living musical experience where melodies and arrangements intertwine with unexpected turns. Beyond electronic music, he plays guitar and keys, writes mostly in English, and always brings improvisation into his sets. At times, it feels as if he’s simply playing with sound, creating something new in the moment.

His music evokes a sense of lightness, like pleasant weather — perhaps sunny, but always filled with hope. Even the lyrical motifs resemble a cloud that eventually dissolves, letting the light through.

KBT is an experimenter exploring the edges of genres, creating music defined by its independence. It doesn’t follow the flow — it carves its own path: isolated, yet magnetic.
